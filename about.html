What is Protect our Schools?

Protect our Schools is a student-led social responsibility campaign aimed to reduce Theft in and around North High School. Through this campaign we will increase awareness around theft, improve surveillance measures, as well as establish an effective reporting system.

Why Protect our Schools?

Recent surveys conducted among students, teachers, and parents revealed:

  • 54% of students reported feeling unsafe about their personal belongings at school.
  • 40% of staff members noted an increase in theft incidents over the past year.
  • 20% higher theft rates in North high Scool compared to neighboring schools

Methodology

Awareness Campaigns:

Conduct workshops and assemblies to educate students on the impacts of theft and the importance of looking out for one another.

Surveillance Enhancements:

Make sure cameras throughout the school are being heavily monitored at all times to reduce incidents

Timeline

  • Month 1-2: Conduct needs assessment and finalize promotional materials.
  • Month 3-4: Launch awareness campaigns and workshops.
  • Month 5: Implement surveillance upgrades.
  • Month 6-12: Evaluate progress and adjust strategies as needed.
